Roots of auxiliary equation: $m=p\pm qi$
$$
\begin{align}
y_1=e^{px}e^{ipx}=e^{px}(\cos(qx)+i\sin(qx)) \\
y_2=e^{px}e^{ipx}=e^{px}(\cos(qx)-i\sin(qx)) \\
\end{align}
$$
All linear combinations of the roots $y_1$ and $y_2$ are solutions: 
$$y_1+y_2=2e^{px}\cos(qx)$$
$$u(x)=\frac12 (y_1+y_2)=e^{px}(\cos(qx)+i\sin (qx))$$
$$y_1-y_2=2ie^{px}\sin(qx)$$
$$v(x)=\frac{1}{2i}(y_1-y_2=e^{px}\sin(qx)$$
$$y=Au(x)+Bv(x)=e^{px}\left( A\cos(qx)+B\sin(qx) \right)$$
